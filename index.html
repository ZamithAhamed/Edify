Inter University Article Competition

The 18th Issue of the most awaited University Business Magazine is coming soon. Grab this wondrous opportunity to publish your ideas on Management, IT and Entrepreneurship in “Exposition”.

Exposition

Exposition Magazine

Issue 17

With the vision of “A Thought Leader in Digital Transformation” the Department of Industrial Management publishes the Exposition Magazine, the most outstanding magazine of the year to bridge the gap between Management and IT platforms. It is one of the leading university business magazines issued to mark the footprints of undergraduates in the corporate world. The legacy unfolds for the 17th consecutive year and for the first time ever we are ready to publish your valuable articles.

Write away to your heart's content and submit your articles to 'Edify,' the first-ever Inter-University article competition of Exposition.

The winning article will be published along with other valuable content, such as interviews with renowned personalities and articles on the latest business / IT trends.

Guidelines

Rules And Regulations


•Consider the themes of IT, Management and Entrepreneurship when writing the article.

•The article should be in English with a word count of approximately 1000-1500 words.

•The content should be in a continuous narrative, not as a list of points.

•The structure of the content should be relatively formal.

•Articles should be well researched and cover unique areas based on your original ideas.

Important


•Plagiarism is prohibited. Copied or reproduced information or facts from the web will not be accepted.

•Facts and claims must be verifiable.

•Opinions should be presented in a fair, objective manner with a focus on the main topic.

•A creative and appropriate topic should be added to the article.

•Articles should be illustrated with relevant images in high-resolution (300 dpi). Graphical tools such as graphs and charts can be used. Avoid clip arts for all purposes.

•The decision of the panel of judges will be final and unalterable.

Submission Guidelines


•Images should be placed in a separate folder and renamed according to the purpose of it in the article (Self-Explanatory).

•All the sources used in the article (references to statistics or quotations used) should be saved in a separate document.

•The article, images folder, and the document of references should all be in one folder ready for submission.

•The link for the article submission will be provided after the registration.
